GRAPHIC DESIGN

Find Out More

 
Graphic design includes planning, analyzing, and creating visual solutions to communication problems. Graphic designers use print, electronic, and film media while using a variety of methods such as color, type, illustration, photography, animation, and various print and layout techniques.
 
 
Graphic designers also develop the overall layout and produstion design of magazines, newspapers, journals, corporate reports, and other publications.
 
 
Graphic designers also produce promotional displays, packaging, marketing brochures for products and services and logos for products and business and develop signs/signage for systems for design, interactive media, multimedia projects, and they also create the opening and closing credits of movies and television programs.
